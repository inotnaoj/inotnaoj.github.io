Who am I?

  • PSYCHOLOGY
  • I hold a degree in Psychology, where my keen interest in research led me to undertake my undergraduate thesis in cognitive research, conducted in collaboration with the University of Barcelona. During my psychology studies, I delved into the fascinating world of primate behavior, contributing to ethological research.
  • ORGANIZATIONAL MANAGEMENT
  • Eager to expand my expertise, I pursued a Master's in Management and Development of People and Teams in Organizations. I have had the privilege of contributing my expertise to distinguished organizations such as NTTDATA and Iberostar Hotels. This journey further fueled my understanding of human dynamics and organizational structures.
  • DATA ANALYSIS|DATA SCIENCE
  • Driven by an insatiable curiosity, I eventually transitioned into the realm of data. I completed a Master's in Behavioral Data Science, where I harnessed my analytical skills to explore the intricate patterns within behavioral data.
  • MASTER'S THESIS: ELEVATING ESPORTS WITH DATA PRECISION
  • I conducted my Master's thesis in collaboration with the University Alfonso X el Sabio, concentrating on the analysis and prediction of professional Esports player performance. This research involved navigating the complexities of Esports analytics, aiming to uncover patterns and insights that contribute to a deeper understanding of player dynamics and strategies.
  • DATA ANALYST|DATA SCIENTIST SPECIALIZATION
  • Specializing in SQL, Python, R, Tableau, and Power BI, I bring a unique blend of psychology and HR expertise to my role as a Data Analyst and Data Scientist. Dedicated to leveraging my technical skills and innovative mindset, my goal is to uncover valuable insights that inform strategic decision-making and contribute to measurable impact in every project I undertake.
